Innovation

At the core of Verasoft is proprietary filament engineering focused on performance optimisation and environmental responsibility.

Unlike conventional PVA yarns, Verasoft is designed to dissolve more efficiently, reducing chemical usage, water load, and processing complexity. Its controlled solubility and consistent filament structure enable manufacturers to unlock advanced constructions while maintaining operational stability.

Our innovation philosophy combines:

  • Material science
  • Process efficiency
  • Sustainability by design

This makes Verasoft a future-ready solution for textile manufacturers worldwide.
