About Us

Atex Global Ventures Limited is an innovation company focused on advancing sustainable solutions for the global textile industry.

We are headquartered in Hong Kong with a branch office in India. We work closely with textile manufacturers and partners to develop yarns that improve yarn performance while supporting eco-friendly and cost-efficient manufacturing practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support the textile industry’s shift toward more sustainable production by developing eco-friendly yarn solutions that deliver reliable performance at scale.

We aim to help manufacturers reduce environmental impact without compromising on quality, efficiency, or consistency.

Our Commitment

Sustainability is central to how we innovate and operate. We’re committed to developing environmentally responsible yarn solutions & building long-term partnerships with textile manufacturers.

Through Verasoft, we continue to shape the future of textiles—one filament at a time.
